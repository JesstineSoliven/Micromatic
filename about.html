About Corematic

Corematic was founded with a simple goal: help businesses work smarter through automation and intelligent digital solutions.

The company started from years of hands-on experience in process automation, workflow optimization, and digital development. Before founding Corematic, the founder worked as a Robotic Process Automation (RPA) developer, building automation solutions that streamline business operations and eliminate repetitive manual work.

Over time, it became clear that many organizations — especially small and growing businesses — spend countless hours on repetitive tasks such as data entry, report preparation, file processing, and system updates. These activities consume valuable time that could instead be spent on growing the business, serving customers, and developing new opportunities.

Corematic was created to bridge that gap. By combining expertise in AI automation, robotic process automation (RPA), workflow automation, and digital design, Corematic helps organizations simplify complex processes and introduce smarter ways of working.

Our Background

The foundation of Corematic comes from real-world experience designing and implementing automation solutions in professional environments. The founder has spent several years developing automation systems using technologies such as:

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) AI-Powered Workflow Tools Microsoft Power Automate Python Automation Frameworks Intelligent Data Processing

These solutions have helped teams reduce manual work, improve efficiency, and streamline operations across multiple business processes.

Prior to working in automation, the founder also worked as a graphic artist, bringing a strong foundation in visual communication and digital design. This unique combination of technology and design allows Corematic to deliver solutions that are both functional and visually effective.

Helping Small Businesses Work Smarter

Many small businesses operate with limited resources, small teams, and growing workloads. Often, employees spend a large portion of their time performing repetitive administrative tasks that could easily be automated.

Corematic helps small businesses by introducing practical automation solutions that:

Reduce repetitive manual work
Improve operational efficiency
Save valuable time for business owners and employees
Reduce human errors in daily processes
Allow teams to focus on higher-value activities

Examples of tasks that can be automated include:

Data entry between systems
Generating reports automatically
Processing emails and attachments
Updating business records
File and document processing
Workflow approvals and notifications

Instead of hiring additional staff to manage repetitive work, businesses can use automation to scale their operations efficiently.
